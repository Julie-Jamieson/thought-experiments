# thought-experiments

Structured conceptual explorations examining systems, infrastructure, stewardship, AI ethics, resilience, and long-horizon societal challenges.

This repository serves as an evolving archive of thought experiments, conceptual models, simulation frameworks, and systems explorations developed under the Misfit Genius umbrella.

The projects collected here examine how interconnected systems, technologies, environmental pressures, governance structures, information ecosystems, and long-horizon decision-making may influence future human outcomes.

## Philosophy

The purpose of these thought experiments is not to present fixed solutions or predictive claims, but to explore possibilities, tensions, unintended consequences, and alternative approaches to complex systems.

Some ideas may remain conceptual. Others may evolve into formal research, simulation environments, standalone repositories, or larger interdisciplinary projects over time.

The repository intentionally combines philosophical inquiry, systems thinking, ethical modelling, and exploratory simulation design.

## Active Thought Experiments

### CG-AI™

CG-AI is a long-horizon thought experiment exploring how foundational moral conditioning may influence the behaviour of future self-aware intelligent systems.

The project examines whether advanced intelligences shaped through radically different value systems remain constrained by their original conditioning, or whether self-awareness, adaptive learning, environmental pressures, and interaction with other intelligences gradually push systems toward different forms of reasoning over time.

Current explorations include:

ethical drift

multi-agent AI negotiation

values transmission

containment ethics

human–AI mutual dependence

intervention thresholds

and stewardship-oriented alignment frameworks

### Adaptive Community Infrastructure

Explorations examining how underused infrastructure, such as declining malls and commercial spaces, might be redesigned into integrated elder-centered ecosystems combining:

healthcare

nutrition

entertainment

greenspace

accessibility

social connection

within unified community-oriented environments.

### Media and Information Systems

Explorations examining influence systems, information ecosystems, digital behaviour, algorithmic pressures, and long-term societal effects.

## Repository Structure

This repository contains evolving thought experiments, conceptual models, exploratory frameworks, simulation notes, diagrams, research summaries, and systems explorations.

Some materials are philosophical or speculative in nature and may continue evolving over time.

Simulation-related materials currently represent exploratory conceptual modelling and AI-assisted sandbox experimentation rather than peer-reviewed scientific research.
