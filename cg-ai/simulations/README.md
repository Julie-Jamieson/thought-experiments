# Simulations

This section contains exploratory simulation frameworks, agent-based modelling concepts, behavioural testing environments, and experimental system architectures related to CG-AI.

Current simulations are primarily conceptual and exploratory in nature and may include:

* NetLogo-based modelling
* multi-agent interaction environments
* resource pressure simulations
* cooperation and conflict scenarios
* constraint reinterpretation experiments
* stewardship-oriented behavioural conditions
* long-horizon systems modelling
* environmental instability testing

Simulation materials may include:

* model frameworks
* experimental configurations
* behavioural rules
* parameter structures
* initialization conditions
* simulation notes
* observational summaries
* iteration tracking

The purpose of this section is to support the transition from conceptual thought experiments into more structured and reproducible simulation environments.

Current simulation work represents exploratory modelling and AI-assisted sandbox experimentation rather than validated scientific simulation or peer-reviewed research.

As the project evolves, simulation environments, assumptions, and methodologies may continue to change significantly.
