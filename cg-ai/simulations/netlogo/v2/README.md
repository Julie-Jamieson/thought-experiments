# NetLogo v2

This section contains the second-generation NetLogo simulation environments developed for CG-AI.

The purpose of the v2 migration is to move earlier conceptual thought experiments and AI-mediated sandbox simulations into more structured agent-based modelling environments.

These simulations explore behavioural dynamics involving:

* cooperation and conflict
* resource pressure
* environmental dependency
* adaptive reasoning
* stewardship-oriented alignment
* long-horizon survivability
* multi-agent interaction

The NetLogo environments within this section are exploratory simulation frameworks intended to improve:

* reproducibility
* environmental modelling
* behavioural consistency
* comparative testing
* experimental transparency

Current simulations remain conceptual and exploratory in nature.

The models contained here should not be interpreted as predictive AGI systems, validated behavioural science, or empirical consciousness research.

Instead, they function as constrained simulation environments designed to examine whether recurring behavioural patterns emerge under differing environmental pressures, incentive structures, and alignment conditions.

This section may contain:

* NetLogo model files
* simulation configurations
* experimental notes
* parameter structures
* preliminary observations
* iteration tracking
* behavioural outcome summaries

As the project evolves, these models may continue to change significantly through ongoing refinement and experimentation.
