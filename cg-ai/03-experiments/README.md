# Experiments

This section contains structured behavioural experiments, exploratory test scenarios, and iterative simulation studies related to CG-AI.

Experiments are designed to examine how intelligent systems may respond under varying conditions, including:

* shared resource pressure
* conflicting objectives
* cooperation incentives
* constraint reinterpretation
* environmental instability
* multi-agent interaction
* long-horizon decision-making
* stewardship-oriented alignment conditions

Each experiment may include:

* objectives
* prompts or initialization conditions
* experimental setup
* behavioural constraints
* observations
* outcomes
* limitations
* revisions or follow-up iterations

Experiments within this repository are exploratory and simulation-oriented in nature.

Observed behaviours should not be interpreted as evidence of artificial consciousness, AGI emergence, or predictive models of future intelligent systems.

The purpose of this section is to document structured exploration, behavioural patterns, emergent tendencies, and experimental uncertainty within constrained environments.
